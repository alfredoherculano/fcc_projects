Wolfgang Güllich

German rock climber

Quick timeline of Güllich's life and achievements

  • 1960 - Born in Ludwigshafen, West Germany
  • 1973 - Starts aid climbing, introduced by his father
  • 1981 - Moves to Frankenjura
  • 1983 - Frees Mister Magnesia, first German 7c+
  • 1982 - First repeat of Grand Illusion (8a) in Lake Tahoe
  • 1984 - World's first 8b redpoint with Kanal im Rücken
  • 1985 - World's first 8b+ redpoint with Punks in the Gym
  • 1986 - Free solos Separate Reality (7a) in Yosemite
  • 1986 - World's first 7c free solo with Weed Killer at Raven Tor
  • 1987 - World's first 8c redpoint with Wallstreet
  • 1989 - First ascent, with Kurt Albert and others, of the big wall Eternal Flame
  • 1991 - First ascent, with Kurt Albert and others, of the big wall climb Riders on the Storm
  • 1991 - Makes history with the world's first 9a redpoint, Action Directe in Frankenjura, which would later be described as "Güllich's Masterpiece"
  • 1992 - Died from a car accident in Germany, at the age of 32

"If you want to explain the fascination of sport climbing, it is not sufficient to refer to the conservative view of sport that describes it purely in terms of athletic performance as an isolated phenomenon. Rather, the particular value in climbing is the adventure aspect and the life style."

-- Wolfgang Güllich
